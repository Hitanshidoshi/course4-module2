Pina Colada

Pina Colada consists of 1 cup pineapple, 1 whole banana, 1 cup of coconut milk and 1 tbsp of shredded coconut. Take all the ingredients and mix them using blender. Serve in a glass and garnish with a piece of pineapple.

Green Dream

Green Dream consists of 1 and half cups of baby spinach, 1 whole banana, 1 cup of frozen mango and juice of 1 lemon. Take all the ingredients and mix them using blender. Serve in a glass and garnish with a piece of lemon.

Razzle Dazzle

Razzle Dazzle consists of 1/2 cup of frozen pineapple, 1/2 cup of frozen mango, 1 cup of coconut milk and 1 cup of frozen raspberries. Take all the ingredients and mix them using blender. Serve in a glass and garnish with pieces of raspberries.
